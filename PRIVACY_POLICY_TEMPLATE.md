# Privacy Policy for FuliTrack

**Last Updated**: [DATE]

## Introduction

FuliTrack ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Fuliza loan tracking application.

**Key Point**: FuliTrack is a privacy-first application. All your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share your personal or financial information with any third parties.

## Information We Access

### SMS Messages (Read Permission)

**What we access**: FuliTrack requests permission to read SMS messages on your device solely to detect and parse Fuliza loan notifications from M-PESA (Safaricom).

**How we use it**:
- We scan incoming SMS messages to identify Fuliza-related messages
- We extract loan details (amount, date, deadline) from these messages
- We store this information locally on your device in a local database

**What we DON'T do**:
- We do NOT read personal messages or conversations
- We do NOT transmit SMS content to any servers
- We do NOT share SMS data with third parties
- We do NOT collect any messages unrelated to Fuliza loans

**Data Storage**: All SMS-derived data is stored locally in your device's app storage using SQLite database. This data never leaves your device.

### Notification Permission

**What we access**: Permission to display local notifications on your device.

**How we use it**:
- To send you reminders about upcoming Fuliza loan payment deadlines
- To notify you when new Fuliza loans are detected

**What we DON'T do**:
- We do NOT send any data through notifications to external services
- All notifications are generated locally on your device

### Storage Permission

**What we access**: Local device storage to save app data.

**How we use it**:
- To store loan history in a local SQLite database
- To save app settings and preferences
- To cache chart data for analytics

**What we DON'T do**:
- We do NOT sync data to cloud storage
- We do NOT access files outside the app's designated storage area

## Data We Store Locally

The following information is stored on your device only:

1. **Loan Information**:
   - Loan amount
   - Disbursement date
   - Repayment deadline
   - Loan status (active/repaid)
   - Transaction dates

2. **App Settings**:
   - Notification preferences
   - Analytics opt-in status
   - Theme preferences (if applicable)

3. **Calculated Analytics**:
   - Total loans taken
   - Average loan amount
   - Repayment patterns
   - Charts and statistics data

**Important**: None of this data is transmitted over the internet or shared with any external services.

## Data We DO NOT Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:
- ❌ Your name or personal identity
- ❌ Phone numbers
- ❌ M-PESA PIN or passwords
- ❌ Bank account information
- ❌ SMS message content (beyond Fuliza notifications)
- ❌ Contact lists
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address, etc.)
- ❌ IP addresses
- ❌ Any personally identifiable information (PII)

## Firebase Analytics (Optional)

FuliTrack includes **optional** Firebase Analytics that is **disabled by default**.

### If You Opt-In to Analytics

If you choose to enable analytics in the app settings:

**What is collected** (anonymous, aggregated data only):
- App usage statistics (screens viewed, features used)
- Performance metrics (app crashes, load times)
- General device information (OS version, device model)

**What is NOT collected** (even with analytics enabled):
- ❌ Financial data (loan amounts, payment information)
- ❌ SMS content or messages
- ❌ Personal identification information
- ❌ User location
- ❌ Advertising identifiers

**How to control**:
- Analytics is OFF by default
- You can enable it in Settings → Privacy & Analytics
- You can disable it at any time

**Purpose**: Analytics help us understand how users interact with the app so we can improve features and fix bugs. All analytics data is anonymous and aggregated.

## No Internet Connection Required

FuliTrack operates entirely offline. The app:
- ✅ Works without an internet connection
- ✅ Does not require account creation or login
- ✅ Does not communicate with external servers (except optional Firebase Analytics if enabled)
- ✅ Stores all data locally on your device

## Data Security

We take your privacy seriously:

1. **Local Storage Only**: All data is stored in your device's protected app storage
2. **Device Encryption**: Data benefits from Android's device-level encryption
3. **No Network Transmission**: Data never leaves your device (except anonymous analytics if opted-in)
4. **No Third-Party Sharing**: We never share your data with anyone

## Your Data Rights

You have complete control over your data:

### Right to Access
- All your data is accessible within the app
- You can view all loan records in the app interface

### Right to Delete
You can delete your data at any time by:
- Deleting individual loan records within the app
- Clearing all data in app settings
- Uninstalling the app (removes all app data)

### Right to Export
- Currently, export features are not available
- All data remains on your device in SQLite format

### Right to Opt-Out
- You can deny SMS permissions (app will not function)
- You can disable notifications
- You can opt-out of analytics (or never opt-in)

## Children's Privacy

FuliTrack is intended for adults (18+) who use Fuliza loans. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has used our app, please contact us.
